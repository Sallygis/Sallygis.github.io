Home Page Wireframe
The home page is often the first page visitors see when they arrive at a website. Its primary intent is to make a positive first impression and capture the attention of visitors. It provides a clear and intuitive navigation structure that helps visitors find their way around the website. This includes menus, links to key sections, and calls to action.
Personality Page Wireframe
A personality page, often referred to as a personal profile or bio, is a webpage or section on a website that provides information about an individual's background, interests, and characteristics. It serves to introduce the person to others, whether for personal, professional, or social purposes. It should also include information about your hobbies, interests, and passions outside of work.
Personal Development Page Wireframe
The purpose of a personal development page focused on how the Twelve Pillars can be applied to my life is to create a platform for self-reflection, growth, and accountability. The page can be used to set specific goals and intentions for personal development based on the Twelve Pillars.
My CV Page Wireframe
The purpose of a page containing your resume is to provide a comprehensive and organized overview of your qualifications, skills, work experience, education, and achievements to potential employers, recruiters, or other individuals interested in your professional background. It serves as a documented record of your work history and achievements, which can be useful for various purposes, including visa applications, grants, or educational opportunities.
